# Impeccable

## Design Context

### Users
Adult nicotine-pouch consumers browsing the Glow brand site, most often on phones (social/ad traffic), some on desktop. Job to be done: quickly feel the brand, explore the two flavor sets and strengths, and register interest. Viewing context skews evening/leisure.

### Brand Personality
Luminous, premium, playful-futuristic. The interface should evoke intrigue and calm confidence — a dark room where the product itself is the light source. Not clinical, not loud.

### Aesthetic Direction
Immersive black-void 3D stage (three.js + bloom), neon flavor-coded accents (mint, citrus, berry, ice, ember, rose), ultra-light typography with wide letter-spacing, scroll-driven cinematic choreography (GSAP ScrollTrigger). Anti-references: generic e-commerce grids, white catalog pages, glassmorphism-everywhere dashboards.

### Design Principles
1. Dark stage, product is the light — glow carries hierarchy, not chrome.
2. One continuous cinematic scroll — no page jumps, motion conveys state.
3. Flavor color is meaning — each accent maps to a real flavor, never decoration.
4. Adapt the stage, don't shrink it — on small screens re-frame the camera and re-fit the choreography; never amputate the 3D experience.
5. Touch is first-class — 44px targets, drag-to-spin, no hover-dependent meaning.
